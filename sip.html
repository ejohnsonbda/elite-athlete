Funding Analysis Dashboard — FY 2026/2027

The Sport Investment Program (SIP), led by Bermuda's Department of Sport & Recreation, strengthens the development of local sports through targeted funding to National Sport Governing Bodies (NSGBs). Guided by the National Sports Policy (2022–2027), the program aims to increase participation, improve performance, and build sustainable athlete pathways.
